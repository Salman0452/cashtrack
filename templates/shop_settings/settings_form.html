How It Works

When you record a Print/Copy transaction:

  • Enter the quantity (number of pages)
  • Select print type (Black & White or Color)
  • Enter the amount charged to customer
  • System calculates: Profit = Amount - (Quantity × Cost per page)
Example: 10 pages B&W, charged Rs. 100
Cost = 10 × Rs. {{ form.print_bw_cost.value|default:"5.00" }} = Rs. {{ form.print_bw_cost.value|default:"5.00"|floatformat:0|default:"5" }}0
Profit = Rs. 100 - Rs. {{ form.print_bw_cost.value|default:"5.00"|floatformat:0|default:"5" }}0 = Rs. {{ form.print_bw_cost.value|default:"5.00"|floatformat:0|default:"5" }}0
